Lassi

Description

Lassi is a cooling, refreshing, probiotic drink perfect for the warm summer months. Popular in Punjab and North India, there are many variations of a lassi recipe, including mango lassi, dry fruits lassi, and malai lassi.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup curd (thick)
  • 2 tbsp pistachios
  • 2 tbsp almonds
  • 1 anjeer (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • ¼ tsp saffron food colour

Steps

  1. firstly, in a blender take 1 cup curd. make sure the curd is thick and chilled.
  2. add 2 tbsp pistachios, 2 tbsp almonds, 1 anjeer, 2 tbsp sugar and ¼ tsp saffron food colour.
  3. blend to make sure everything is well combined.
  4. finally, pour dry fruit lassi in a tall glass and top with chopped pistachios.
